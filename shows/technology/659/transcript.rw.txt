tuzagenda byoroshye kuri wewe ariko ariko urakoze cyane kubwinkunga yawe
no kuduha amahirwe yo gukora iki gitaramo ndashaka kuvuga ko ari amahirwe nkaya
kuba kuruhande rwa kamera no kumenya umuryango wacu binyuze iwacu
seriveri idahwitse kimwe nicyumweru cyiza cyane ndakeka abantu bose kandi tuzabikora
kongera kukubona vuba witonde
